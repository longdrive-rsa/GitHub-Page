Request Account Deletion

This page allows users of LongDrive to request deletion of their account and associated personal data.


How to request deletion

  1. Show the account deletion form below
  2. Enter your name, surname, email address and phone number associated with your account
  3. Submit the request
  4. Your account will be deleted within 30 days after verification

Data deleted

  • Username
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • First name and surname
  • ID number e.t.c

Data retained

No personal data is retained after account deletion, except where required by law.

Please complete the form below to request deletion of your Long Drive SA account.

By submitting this request, you confirm that you are the account holder. Requests are processed within 30 days.
