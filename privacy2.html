Privacy Policy

We dedicate our time to give our users the best experience when they use our services. One way in which we do this is by collecting users data and processing it to give aggregated insights of our customers.

We receive, collect and store any information you enter on our website or provide us in any other way. We collect personally identifiable information which includes names, phone number, email etc.

We may use software tools to measure and collect session information, including page response times, length of visits to certain pages, page interaction information, and number of visits.


When you fill out the subscribe form on our website, we collect your email address. When you fill out a form in the chat, we collect your name, email address and your phone number.

The information that will be requested will only be limited to the data we use for user experience. Your personal information will only be used for the specific reasons stated below.


We collect this information for the following reasons:

  • For communication; using the details you have provided, we're able to get in contact with you for a response, feedback or when it's necessary to contact you.
  • To provide our users with ongoing customer assistance and technical support.
  • To create aggregated statistical data that is not subject to person. This gives us feedback on how our customers interact with the website and thus help us improve our services.

All information collected is stored and kept in the company database. We have placed measures to ensure that there's no breach to our stored data and your personal information is not shared with third parties.


We will contact you using the details you have provided i.e. email address or phone number for various reasons which include promotions, to give you feedback, to resolve a dispute or for marketing campaign if you have filled the subscribe form.

You may unsubscribe to our emails if you no longer want to receive them. Alternatively you can send us an email with your details and we'll be able to help you.


We reserve the right to modify this privacy policy at any time, so please review it frequently. Changes in our privacy policy will take effect upon their posting on the website. We'll let our customers know of any material changes in our policy.


For any changes in the details provided or to opt out please send us an email on info@longdrivesa.com.
